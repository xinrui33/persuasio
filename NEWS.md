# persuasio 0.0.9

* Initial CRAN submission.
* `persuasio()` unified wrapper for all estimators.
* `aprlb()` and `aprub()` for the average persuasion rate.
* `lpr4ytz()` for the local persuasion rate.
* `calc4persuasio()` for bounds from summary statistics.
* `persuasio4ytz()`, `persuasio4yz()`, and `persuasio4ytz2lpr()` for inference wrappers.
* GKB dataset included.
